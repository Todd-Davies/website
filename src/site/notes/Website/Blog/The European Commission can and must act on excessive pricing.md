Originally published on [**ProMarket**](https://www.promarket.org/2025/10/08/the-european-commission-can-and-must-act-on-excessive-pricing/), along with Aline Blankertz, Justine Haekens and Nicholas Shaxson.

> The European Commission has struggled to tackle excessive prices in Europe, despite evidence of how they arise in relation to market power. We argue that adopting accounting and financial analysis as part of its toolkit can enable the Commission to understand and act when firms are exploiting their market power.
