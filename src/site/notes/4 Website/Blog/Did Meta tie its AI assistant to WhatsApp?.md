> Meta recently integrated its AI assistant, Meta AI, into WhatsApp, Instragram and Facebook. This post, which grew into [this paper](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5375544), analyses whether Meta could have abused its dominant position under competition law by doing so.
